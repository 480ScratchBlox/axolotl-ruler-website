Axolotl Ruler Reaction Roles

This page will tell you what each reaction role is based off so you could decide what announcements you want to recieve.

  • General announcements are just the announcements that apply to pretty much anyone in this server, basically it is just the @everyone announcements now under this role.
  • Youtube announcements are announcements related to any Youtube content, that is pretty much it.
  • Youtube Sapphire announcements are announcements created and automated by Sapphire. This role is good for getting notified relatively to when a new Youtube video is uploaded.
  • Help announcements are used to call people to help with something for Axolotl Ruler. Most scripted videos fall under this role.
  • Axolotl Ruler game announcements are announcements regarding games that Axolotl Ruler developed/worked on, most notable the Nerf Miner Obby.
  • Game announcements notify information about certain video games present in this Discord server.
  • Miscellaneous announcements are announcements that do not really fit in any role. It can be quite the surprising role.
